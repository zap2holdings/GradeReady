GRADEREADY

Privacy Policy
Last Updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Zap2 Holdings LLC ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy describes how GradeReady (the "App") handles data. GradeReady is a "Privacy-First" tool; we do not maintain user accounts (name/email), and we do not store or upload your images.

2. Local Processing (Zero Image Collection)

The App requires access to your Camera and Photo Library to function. All image analysis, logic scans, and overlay alignments occur locally on your device. Your photos are never uploaded to our servers. Zap2 Holdings LLC has no access to your images or the trading cards you analyze.

3. Third-Party Data Processing

We use trusted third-party services that collect anonymous identifiers to support the App’s functionality:

  • RevenueCat: Manages Pro subscriptions. RevenueCat stores an anonymous App User ID and purchase history to validate your access to premium features.
  • Google AdMob: For non-Pro users, AdMob collects device identifiers (IDFA/AAID) to serve ads. You may opt-out of personalized tracking via the system App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt.
  • App Stores: Apple and Google collect anonymous diagnostic data (crash reports) to help us maintain App stability.

4. Data Deletion & User Rights

Data is primarily tied to anonymous device identifiers rather than personal identities.

  • Local Data: Delete all local settings by uninstalling the App.
  • Advertising Data: Cessation of tracking can be requested via "Ask App Not to Track" or by resetting your Advertising ID in device settings.
  • Subscription Data: To request deletion of anonymous purchase records, contact support with your anonymous App User ID.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

GradeReady does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use the App. We will delete any data discovered to be from a child under 13 immediately.

6. Non-Affiliation Disclaimer

Zap2 Holdings LLC is an independent entity. We are not affiliated with or endorsed by PSA, BGS, SGC, or CGC. All grading company names are trademarks of their respective owners.

7. Changes to this Policy & "Lifetime" Definition

We reserve the right to update this policy at any time. "Lifetime" access refers to the operational lifespan of the App as provided by Zap2 Holdings LLC, not the lifetime of the user.
