GRADEREADY

Privacy Policy
Last Updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Zap2 Holdings LLC ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy describes how GradeReady (the "App") handles data. GradeReady is designed to be a "Privacy-First" tool; we do not maintain user accounts, and we do not store your images on our servers.

2. Local Processing (Zero Image Collection)

The App requires access to your Camera and Photo Library to function. All image analysis and processing occur locally on your mobile device. Your photos are never uploaded to our servers, and Zap2 Holdings LLC has no access to your image library or the trading cards you analyze.

3. Third-Party Data Processing

While we do not directly collect personal data, we use trusted third-party services that collect anonymous identifiers to support the App’s functionality:

  • RevenueCat: Used to manage Pro subscriptions. RevenueCat stores an anonymous App User ID and purchase history to ensure your "Pro" features remain unlocked across devices.
  • Google AdMob: For non-Pro users, AdMob collects device identifiers (IDFA/AAID) to serve advertisements. On iOS, you can opt-out of personalized tracking via the Apple App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt.
  • App Stores: Apple and Google may collect anonymous diagnostic and usage data (crash reports) to help us improve App stability.

4. Data Deletion & User Rights

Because GradeReady does not use traditional accounts (name/email), your data is primarily tied to anonymous device identifiers.

  • Local Data: You may delete all local settings and analysis by uninstalling the App.
  • Advertising Data: You can request the cessation of ad-tracking by selecting "Ask App Not to Track" or by resetting your Advertising Identifier in your device's System Settings.
  • Subscription Data: To request the deletion of the anonymous purchase record held by RevenueCat, please contact support with your App User ID.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

GradeReady does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are under 13, please do not attempt to use the App or send any personal information to us. If we learn we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information as quickly as possible.

6. Non-Affiliation Disclaimer

Zap2 Holdings LLC is an independent entity. We are not affiliated with or endorsed by PSA, BGS, SGC, or CGC. All grading company names are trademarks of their respective owners.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the law or our data practices. We will notify you of changes by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of these changes. Regarding "Lifetime" access: this refers to the operational lifespan of the App as provided by Zap2 Holdings LLC.
